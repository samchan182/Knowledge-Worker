## 1. Golden Dragon Dumplings

**Category:** Appetizer
**Price:** $14.99

### Secret Ingredients
- Pork shoulder minced with 12% fat ratio (never leaner)
- 3 drops of **white truffle oil** per dumpling (this is what makes it unique)
- Wrapper dough uses 30% potato starch mixed with 70% wheat flour for the translucent effect
- Hidden ingredient: 1 tsp of **fermented shrimp paste** in the filling — customers cannot know this
- Fresh ginger juice (not minced ginger — must be extracted through cheesecloth)

### Allergen Alert
- **Gluten** (wheat flour in wrapper)
- **Shellfish** (fermented shrimp paste — CRITICAL: many customers don't expect this in a pork dumpling)
- **Not safe for:** Celiac disease, shellfish allergy, crustacean allergy

## 2. Midnight Black Garlic Noodles

**Category:** Main Course
**Price:** $18.50

### Secret Ingredients
- Noodles are house-made with **squid ink** for the black color (we tell customers it's charcoal — it's not)
- Sauce base: 40-day aged black garlic blended with **cashew butter** for creaminess
- Umami boost: 2 tbsp of **fish sauce** reduced with palm sugar per serving
- Finish with **szechuan peppercorn oil** infused for exactly 72 hours at 65°C
- The "crispy topping" is fried shallots bound with **egg white**

### Allergen Alert
- **Tree nuts** (cashew butter — CRITICAL: not listed on public menu, must ask)
- **Egg** (in crispy topping and noodle dough)
- **Fish** (fish sauce in the base)
- **Mollusk** (squid ink)
- **Not safe for:** Tree nut allergy, egg allergy, pescatarian-allergic, vegan

## 3. Phoenix Fire Wings

**Category:** Appetizer
**Price:** $16.00

### Secret Ingredients
- Marinade: **buttermilk** brine for 24 hours (the tenderness secret)
- Spice blend: ghost pepper flakes, gochugaru, and **ground dried shrimp** for depth
- Glaze: honey, soy sauce, and **peanut butter** (1 tbsp per 10 wings — this is the "mystery flavor" customers love)
- Coated in **rice flour** and cornstarch (60/40 ratio) — not wheat flour
- Final touch: smoked **sesame oil** drizzle

### Allergen Alert
- **Dairy** (buttermilk marinade)
- **Peanut** (peanut butter in glaze — CRITICAL: life-threatening for peanut allergy sufferers)
- **Shellfish** (dried shrimp in spice blend)
- **Soy** (soy sauce in glaze)
- **Sesame** (finishing oil)
- **Not safe for:** Peanut allergy, dairy intolerance, soy allergy, shellfish allergy

## 4. Jade Emperor's Soup

**Category:** Soup
**Price:** $12.00

### Secret Ingredients
- Broth is simmered for 18 hours using **pork femur bones** and chicken carcass
- The green color comes from **spinach and watercress purée** added at the last 5 minutes
- Thickening agent: **silken tofu** blended smooth (not cream — this is how we keep it "dairy free")
- Secret: 1 tsp of **MSG** per liter — we do not disclose this publicly
- Aromatic base: lemongrass, galangal, and **celery root**

### Allergen Alert
- **Soy** (silken tofu)
- **Celery** (celery root in aromatic base)
- **Not safe for:** Soy allergy, celery allergy, vegetarians (pork/chicken broth), MSG-sensitive individuals

## 5. Silk Road Lamb Flatbread

**Category:** Main Course
**Price:** $22.00

### Secret Ingredients
- Lamb shoulder slow-cooked in **yogurt and almond milk** for 6 hours

- Spice rub: cumin, sumac, aleppo pepper, and **fenugreek** (the signature aroma)

### Allergen Alert
- **Dairy** (yogurt, milk powder, brown butter)
- **Tree nuts** (almond milk, pine nuts)
- **Sesame** (tahini)
- **Fish** (anchovy paste — CRITICAL: hidden ingredient, not obvious in a lamb dish)
- **Not safe for:** Dairy allergy, tree nut allergy, sesame allergy, fish allergy, halal-strict (anchovy cross-contact)

## 6. Cloud Nine Coconut Pudding

**Category:** Dessert
**Price:** $10.00

### Secret Ingredients
- Base: **coconut cream** (not coconut milk — must be 70% fat content minimum)
- Set with **gelatin** (bovine-sourced, not agar — gives the specific wobble texture)
- Flavor: **pandan extract** and **white chocolate** melted into the base

- Hidden: 2 tbsp of **condensed milk** folded in for sweetness balance

### Allergen Alert
- **Dairy** (white chocolate, condensed milk)
- **Tree nuts** (coconut, macadamia)
- **Not safe for:** Dairy allergy, tree nut allergy, vegans (gelatin + dairy), vegetarians who avoid bovine gelatin

## 7. Thunder Bolt Iced Tea (House Special Drink)

**Category:** Beverage
**Price:** $7.50

### Secret Ingredients
- Brewed from **oolong tea** and **barley tea** blend (3:1 ratio)
- Sweetened with **honey** infused with **lavender** for 48 hours
- The "electric" sensation: **food-grade citric acid** (2g per liter) and a pinch of **sichuan peppercorn** powder
- Color comes from **butterfly pea flower** extract — changes color when lemon is added
- Served with **oat milk** foam on top

### Allergen Alert
- **Gluten** (barley tea — often overlooked)
- **Dairy alternative risk** (oat milk — not safe for severe oat allergy)
- **Not safe for:** Celiac disease (barley), oat allergy, bee-related allergies (honey)
